Waystone Table

The Waystone table is a collaboration with some good friends of mine over at Waystone Wargaming Speakeasy. They needed a set of four custom tables dedicated to playing Warhammer and other tabletop games on.

The tables are made from solid cherry and can be converted into several "modes" for different purposes. When playing a game, the table top is inset and an accessory panel provides additional storage area flush with the wide table border. The table top surface can also be raised to be flush with the border to maximize surface area for activities like painting minis. The accessory panels stash away underneath the table when not in use.

The inset and tapered legs along with the beveled table edge and apron detail provide some subtle Art Deco styling to fit the speakeasy aesthetic of the space they live in.

Materials: Cherry, Birch (internal structure), MDO Plywood (table top)

Dimensions: 77" Wide by 53" Deep by 36" Tall
